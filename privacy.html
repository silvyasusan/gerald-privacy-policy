Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Gerald is a Discord bot designed for immersive, story-driven gameplay. This Privacy Policy outlines how Gerald handles data within Discord servers and ensures compliance with Discord's developer policies and privacy standards.

2. What Data Is Collected

Gerald may collect and store the following data strictly for game and moderation purposes:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Publicly shared messages and commands used with the bot
  • Game-related data such as inventory, progress, faction status, and balance

Gerald does not collect or store:

  • Private messages (DMs)
  • Email addresses, passwords, or other personal identifiable information

3. How Data Is Used

All collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Enable gameplay mechanics and features
  • Track player progress and choices
  • Assist moderators in maintaining game integrity

Data is not shared, sold, or transmitted to any third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely in a local or cloud-based SQLite database. Access to the database is restricted to the developer(s) of the bot.

5. Data Removal

If you would like your data removed from Gerald's records, you may contact the server administrator or the bot developer. Requests will be fulfilled as soon as reasonably possible.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in functionality or compliance. The latest version will always be accessible at this page.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, please contact the bot developer through the support server or directly on Discord.
